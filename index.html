About Us

As the Prefects' Guild of Eheliyagoda Central College, our primary objectives are:

  • Our Vision

    Our main vision is to provide proper guidance and support to the entire student community by setting an example of leadership.

  • Maintaining Discipline

    We are dedicated to maintaining the school's discipline at a high level and encouraging students to wear the uniform correctly and neatly.

  • Core Duties

    We are committed to performing our duties at the main gate, during morning assemblies, and at all other special school events.

History of Prefects' Guild

Here you can add the detailed history of the Prefects' Guild of Eheliyagoda Central College as bullet points:

  • Establishment: Founded in 1988 with the aim of maintaining school discipline.
  • First Head Prefect: Hansaka Fernando (1988).
  • Key Milestones:
    • 1995: Introduced new student leadership programs.
    • 2002: Organized the first inter-house prefects' competition.
    • 2010: Launched a community service initiative.
    • 2018: Celebrated 30 years of service with a grand event.
    • 2023: Implemented a digital prefect management system.
  • Evolution of Role: Over the years, the role of prefects has evolved to include fostering student welfare and promoting academic excellence.

About Our Mission

We are a team of passionate and innovative students from Eheliyagoda Central College, brought together by a shared vision of leveraging technology to improve our school's ecosystem.

About The Project

Welcome to the official portal for the Eheliyagoda Central College Prefect Management System (ECCPMS). This project represents a significant leap forward in digitizing and streamlining the responsibilities and tracking of our esteemed Prefects' Guild.

Project Version

Current Version: 1.0.0 (Initial Release). This is the first official release of the ECCPMS. As we continue to develop and improve the platform, new versions with more features and enhancements will be released.

User Guide

Step 1: Logging In

Access the system by using your official credentials on the Login Page. Upon successful login, you will be directed to your personal dashboard.

Step 2: Navigating the Dashboard

Use the sidebar on the left to navigate between different sections. You can get a quick summary on the 'Overview' page, or dive into detailed records for 'Attendance', 'Points', and 'Performance'.

Step 3: Checking Your Profile

Your basic information is displayed at the top of the sidebar. For more details, navigate to the 'My Profile' section.

Step 4: Reporting an Absence

If you are marked absent, a form will become active in the 'Attendance' section. You can select a reason from the dropdown or provide a custom one to inform the administrators.

Step 5: Getting Help

If you encounter any issues or have questions, use the chat widget at the bottom right of the screen to contact an administrator for real-time support.
